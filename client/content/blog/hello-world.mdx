# Hello, World

Welcome to my slice of the internet. This site is a personal project, a place to write, share photos, log what I'm watching and cooking, and keep a record of my professional journey.

## Why build this?

I've always loved the old web — the handcrafted HTML pages, the raw aesthetic, the sense that someone *made* something. Modern web design has gotten incredibly polished, but sometimes I miss the personality.

This site is my attempt to blend the best of both worlds: the Y2K chrome aesthetic I grew up with, wrapped in modern tooling.

<Callout type="note">
This site is built with Next.js 16, TypeScript, and MDX. Blog posts like this one are written in markdown with React components sprinkled in.
</Callout>

## What to expect here

- **Blog posts** about software, design, and whatever else catches my attention
- **Photography** from travels and everyday life
- **Living life** — a log of recently listened music, movies, and dishes I've cooked
- **About** — my professional background
